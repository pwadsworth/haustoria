# World Structure Spec

## Level 1 — Surface Gardens
- Tutorial area
- Easy enemies
- Frequent light/water
- Minimal parkour

Boss:
- Dragonfly

## Level 2 — Underground
- Medium difficulty
- Increased darkness
- More Haustoria usage

Bosses:
- Burrowyrm
- Aphid Swarm
- Wasp Duo

## Level 3 — Flesh Tunnels
- Permanent darkness
- Advanced movement
- Heavy Haustoria dependence

Bosses:
- Daikon Maggot
- Bell Beast

## Day/Night Cycles

### Level 1
- 6 min day
- 4 min night

### Level 2
- 3 min day
- 7 min night

### Level 3
No true daytime.
